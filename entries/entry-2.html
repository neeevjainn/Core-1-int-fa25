MERCURY

Astrological Significance

In astrology, the Sun represents the conscious ego, the self and its expression, personal power, pride and authority, leadership qualities and the principles of creativity, spontaneity, health and vitality, the life force.

It is the core of your potential and uniqueness as an individual. The Sun's placement in a zodiac sign reveals the primal urges and the fundamental motivations of an individual. It signifies how we shine our light into the world.

Astronomical Facts

The Sun is the star at the center of the Solar System. It is a nearly perfect ball of hot plasma, heated to incandescence by nuclear fusion reactions in its core. The Sun radiates this energy mainly as light, ultraviolet, and infrared radiation, and is the most important source of energy for life on Earth.

Its diameter is about 1.39 million kilometers (864,000 miles), or 109 times that of Earth. Its mass is about 330,000 times that of Earth, making up about 99.86% of the total mass of the Solar System.
