Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is often called Earth's "sister planet" due to their similar size and mass. However, the conditions are vastly different; Venus has a dense atmosphere consisting mostly of carbon dioxide, trapping heat in a runaway greenhouse effect that makes it the hottest planet in the solar system. The surface of Venus is a dry desertscape interspersed with slab-like rocks and periodically resurfaced by volcanism. It rotates in the opposite direction to most other planets, meaning the Sun rises in the west and sets in the east.

Venus represents the principles of attraction, love, beauty, and value. In the natal chart, it dictates what we enjoy, our aesthetic tastes, how we relate to others romantically, and our attitude toward money and possessions. It is the force of harmony and cooperation. While the Moon reflects our emotional needs, Venus reflects our emotional desires and pleasures. It governs the arts, fashion, social graces, and the ability to compromise. A prominent Venus creates a charismatic and peace-loving nature.

Stellar Trivia

A day on Venus (243 Earth days) is longer than a year on Venus (225 Earth days). It rotates incredibly slowly and in retrograde motion!
